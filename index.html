Before you begin

What is
Embodied Interaction?

The concept

Embodied interaction is the idea that our bodies are not just input devices — they are part of how we think and decide. Rather than tapping a screen, you use physical movement: a tilt, a lift, a shake. The gesture becomes the choice.

In Motion Match, each question is answered through a different movement. Your phone reads your motion. The result is a date night you physically shaped.
